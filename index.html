Super State Racer

Redefining the automotive industry. That is the goal of the Super State Racer.
The hybrid energy storage system consisting of both a lithium battery pack and ultracapacitor bank is looking to make more efficient EVs that can extend the lifecyle of lithium batteries. Our team is currently converting a combustion go kart to a proof of concept vehicle under my leadership, and seek to impact the research community with our published findings.
